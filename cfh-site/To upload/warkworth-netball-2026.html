We're a locally run, volunteer-driven club dedicated to providing netball for our community. We support junior teams (ages 5–11) competing at Warkworth Showgrounds and Wellsford Centennial Park on Tuesdays and Saturdays, as well as adult teams playing in Wellsford on Friday nights. Your support goes directly towards fees for those who can't afford them, player of the day prizes, bibs, balls, uniforms, first aid kits, and our end of year prizegiving.
