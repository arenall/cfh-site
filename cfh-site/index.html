The problem

Every season. The same four people. The same exhausting events.

NZ sports clubs work incredibly hard for their fundraising — and still barely break even. Here's why:

🌭
Sausage sizzles and bake sales take a full day of volunteer time and raise a few hundred dollars before expenses. There's a ceiling, and everyone knows it.
📞
Approaching local businesses with no professional page, no tax receipt, no follow-through feels awkward. Most say no.
🎟
Raffle after raffle exhausts the same small pool of committee members — the same people, every season, burning out.

Real money left on the table — local businesses who genuinely want to support your club have never been asked properly.
👋
Sound familiar?

Why businesses say yes to your club

We give local businesses real reasons to back you.

The reason most sponsorship approaches fail isn't the relationship — it's the ask. No receipt, no recognition, no follow-through. CFH fixes all three, so when your club reaches out, businesses have every reason to say yes.

🧾
Automatic tax receipts remove the friction
Every business sponsor receives a donation tax receipt the moment they give — automatically, with no chasing required. That removes the single biggest hesitation for businesses considering a donation.
🏆
Real recognition keeps them invested
Their logo appears on your club's page from day one. Badges, certificates, and social shoutouts follow. Businesses who feel publicly acknowledged are far more likely to give again next season.
📋
An impact report closes the loop
At season end, every sponsor receives a professional summary of what their contribution achieved. It makes your club look organised, and gives businesses a reason to stay involved year after year.
🤝
They're hearing from people they already know
CFH doesn't cold-call businesses on your behalf. Your club makes the approach — to your network, in your community. CFH just makes sure that when you ask, it looks professional and gives them an easy way to say yes.
📣
CFH publicly celebrates them — to their own audience
Every donation triggers a social media recognition post from CFH within 24 hours — tagging the business, naming your club, reaching their followers. Businesses get visibility they can't buy. It makes them look good. And it gives them a real reason to give again next season — because now the whole community is watching.
