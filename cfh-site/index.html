What CFH builds for your club

Here's exactly what we deliver — before you go live.

Everything below is built for you in the first 3 days. You don't design, write, or research anything from scratch.

Deliverable 01
🖥️

Your own professional sponsorship page

A custom-branded page for your club — your logo, your tiers, your club's story. Businesses click the link, choose their sponsorship level, and contribute securely in under 5 minutes.

No forms to email back. No bank transfers to chase. No awkward follow-ups. This is what makes your ask feel credible — because the page looks serious and professional, businesses take it seriously.

Deliverable 02 · AI-powered
🤖

A researched list of local businesses to approach

One of the biggest problems clubs face is exhausting their immediate network quickly. We use AI to research local businesses in your specific suburb — tradies, retailers, cafes, professionals — who are most likely to want to support a club like yours.

You own the outreach and make the approach yourself. We make sure you're not missing anyone obvious in your own backyard.

✦ Researched specifically for your suburb and sport
Deliverable 03
✉️

Outreach templates, ready to copy and send

A personalised email, WhatsApp message, and social media post — written specifically for your club. Copy, paste, send. The writing is already done.

Because every template links directly to your live CFH page, any business that opens the message can read the sponsorship tiers, make a decision, and contribute — without a single follow-up from you.

Deliverable 04
⚙️

All the admin — done automatically

The moment a business contributes, they receive their tax receipt and digital sponsor badge — no action required from you. Sponsor logos and recognition go up immediately on your sponsorship page, even optional social shout-outs so businesses see their visibility from day one.

At programme close, sponsors receive a summary of outcomes — what was achieved and how their contribution helped. We tailor this with you during setup.

Deliverable 05 · The difference-maker
📣

We actively incentivise your sponsors to give more — and come back

Most fundraising platforms stop at the receipt. CFH goes further: every business donation triggers a public social media recognition post within 24 hours — tagging the business by name, celebrating their support, reaching their own followers. Businesses love it. It makes them look good in their community, to their clients, and ahead of their competitors.

As CFH grows, businesses who sponsor multiple clubs earn CFH Community Status — a public, cross-platform recognition tier that celebrates their cumulative community contribution. "Diamond Community Sponsor — backing 12 local clubs this season." That's the kind of recognition businesses can't buy anywhere else.

For your club, this means one thing: your sponsors don't just give once — CFH gives them real reasons to give more, give again, and tell other businesses to do the same.

Our story

Built by someone who sat on that committee.

I spent every Saturday for a season on sausage sizzles. The club was still broke — and the businesses who would have backed us were never even asked properly.

CFH started as a frustration. I was the treasurer for a local sports club, and no matter how hard we worked at fundraising, we couldn't get ahead. The same tired events, burned-out volunteers, real money left on the table every season.

The businesses in our community wanted to support local sport. They told me so when I actually asked them properly. They just needed a professional way to do it — a tax receipt, some recognition, something that felt credible.

That's CFH. A platform that makes community sport sponsorship as easy and professional as it deserves to be — for the clubs who need the money, and the businesses who genuinely want to give it.

JR
James Renall
Founder · Community Fundraising Hub · Caring Father
